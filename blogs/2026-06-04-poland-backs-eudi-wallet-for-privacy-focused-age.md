Poland has enacted legislation requiring stricter age verification for adult websites while recommending the European Digital Identity Wallet as a privacy-preserving solution. The government plans to launch anonymous age verification services by end of 2026, potentially integrated into its existing mObywatel digital identity application. This approach aligns with broader European efforts to position digital wallets as the foundation for age assurance while protecting user privacy.
