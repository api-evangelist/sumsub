French and Spanish authorities, with Europol support, shut down a fraudulent document operation in Alicante on May 27, 2026, arresting one suspect and seizing approximately 800 forged European identity documents along with production equipment. The operation revealed an online marketplace that supplied fake identity and administrative documents in physical and digital formats to customers across Europe. Authorities determined the criminal network used industrial-scale production methods to support migrant smuggling operations and help people illegally circumvent border controls and claim false residence rights within the EU.
