The U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission has elevated digital assets to a strategic priority in its draft plan spanning fiscal years 2026-2030. The agency aims to establish a firm regulatory foundation for blockchain technologies while addressing how crypto markets have expanded faster than existing rules. The strategy emphasizes closer coordination between the SEC and Commodity Futures Trading Commission to clarify regulatory boundaries and support market development alongside investor protection.
