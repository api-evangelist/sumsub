Research shows that 47% of adults aged 18-28 plan to bet during the 2026 FIFA World Cup, compared to 31% overall, with one in eight younger bettors reporting previous targeting by fraud schemes. Regulators in France and South Korea have launched awareness campaigns and enforcement measures to combat betting-related fraud ahead of the tournament.
