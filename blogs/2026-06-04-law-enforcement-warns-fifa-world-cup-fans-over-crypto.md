The Los Angeles County Sheriff's Department and FBI have alerted the public about fraudulent schemes targeting 2026 World Cup enthusiasts seeking tickets and merchandise. Criminals are operating counterfeit ticketing websites and social media advertisements designed to harvest personal information and payment details. Both agencies recommend fans purchase directly from official FIFA channels while avoiding suspicious offers requesting payment through cryptocurrency, wire transfers, or gift cards.
