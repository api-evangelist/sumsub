Gaming organizations and labor unions petitioned the US Senate on June 16 to amend the CLARITY Act and prohibit prediction market event contracts tied to sports and casino gaming, arguing platforms like Kalshi and Polymarket circumvent traditional betting regulations. The coalition, including the American Gaming Association and Indian Gaming Association, contends these platforms effectively expanded gambling nationwide without state approval.
